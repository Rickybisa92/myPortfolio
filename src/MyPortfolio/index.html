About me

Hello, I'm Ricardo, a web development enthusiast with a special interest in frontend. My goal is to create visually appealing and functional experiences, where every detail matters. I enjoy the process of bringing ideas to life, combining creativity and technology to transform concepts into interfaces that are both beautiful and usable.

Outside of tech, I'm passionate about health and fitness. I'm dedicated to weightlifting and gymnastics training, and I also enjoy escaping to the mountains whenever I can to disconnect and recharge. My free time often includes quality moments with my dog, who’s a big part of my life, and I put a lot of emphasis on balanced nutrition to keep both mind and body strong. I'm constantly seeking new ways to grow—not only as a developer but also in every area of my life—committed to my well-being, both personal and professional.
